Wayfinding - Signage
Vehicle side and head signs clearly display 4" ADA-compliant route names with origin and destination details visible from multiple sight lines.

 

Safety - Wheels and Braking
Traveling up to 40 miles per hour, KSW-0126 alloy brake pads bring the vehicle to a smooth stop, add ride stability and 3% reduced wear on parts.

 

Comfort - Climate Control
Every vehicle has its own central air conditioning unit that use 16% more circulated fresh air and responsive climate controlled temperatures.

 

Safety - Lighting
Each vehicle uses Light Emitting Diode (LED) Halogen JS16-G lamps that last significantly longer and use over 80% less power.

 

Comfort - Interiors
